Health & Wellness

Tips From Our Team

Practical, everyday advice — for anything more specific, our pharmacists are always happy to chat.

Winter Skin & Cold Season

Cold, dry air can leave skin cracked and irritated. Moisturise right after bathing while skin is still damp, keep a lip balm handy, and drink water regularly — dehydration shows on skin before it shows anywhere else.

Baby Care Basics

Change nappies frequently to prevent rash, and let skin air-dry for a few minutes before redressing baby. If a rash doesn't improve within a couple of days despite regular changes and barrier cream, it's worth having a pharmacist or doctor take a look.

Staying on Top of Chronic Medication

Try linking your refill date to something routine, like the 1st of the month, so it's easy to remember. If you're ever unsure whether it's time to reorder, our Track My Order tool or a quick WhatsApp to us can confirm your last order date.

When to Seek Help Urgently

Difficulty breathing, chest pain, sudden severe allergic reactions, or symptoms that rapidly worsen need immediate medical attention — please go straight to a doctor, clinic, or emergency services rather than waiting. For urgent medication needs outside these situations, our Emergency Assist button is here to help.

These tips are general information only and aren't a substitute for professional medical advice. For anything specific to your health or medication, please speak to one of our pharmacists.
